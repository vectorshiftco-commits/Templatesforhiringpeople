Senior Product Designer
📍San Francisco, CA ⏱Full-Time 🏢Hybrid · 3 days onsite 🎓3+ Years Experience
About the Role
We're looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our growing team at NexaVentures. In this role, you'll shape the way our users interact with cutting-edge tools used by over 2 million professionals worldwide. You'll work at the intersection of strategy, research, and craft — turning complex problems into elegant, intuitive experiences that people love.
What You'll Do
  • Lead end-to-end design for core product features, from discovery through delivery
  • Collaborate with Product, Engineering, and Data teams to define solutions that drive business outcomes
  • Conduct user research, interviews, and usability tests to inform design decisions
  • Maintain and evolve our design system, ensuring consistency across all touchpoints
  • Mentor junior designers and help raise the bar for design quality across the org
What We're Looking For
  • 5+ years of product design experience at a technology company
  • Mastery of Figma and a strong understanding of design systems
  • Portfolio demonstrating complex problem-solving and high-quality visual craft
  • Experience with user research methods and translating insights into design
  • Excellent communication skills and comfort presenting to senior stakeholders
Bonus Points
  • Experience designing for B2B SaaS or data-heavy products
  • Familiarity with front-end development (HTML/CSS, React)
  • Motion design or prototyping with advanced interactions
